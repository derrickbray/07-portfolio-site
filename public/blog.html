Day 1

Our first day was a bit rough. We were introduced to The IronYard by trying to fix extremely broken code. Our Instruction built a website and then took a sledgehammer to it. He removed class names, removed the links, and made many syntax errors. We receivedthe assignment late in the afternoon and told to fix it by tomorrow.

Day 1

Our introduction to JavaScript was to build a blackjack game. The app would tell the player to stay, hit, or split based on the cards it was given. It is a very simple game but full of loops and if/else statements. It was hard for the first day, but very cool. It was amazing to see a few lines of code move and manipulate data like that.

Day 2

In week 4 we had a group project with the back-end class to make a ping pong game tracker. Our front-end group had to communicate and work with a database that was built for us. It was interesting to see how much non-coding work had to be put into the project. We spent a lot of time just talking about how the app should work and what it should look like. It was a great opportunity to understand how real projects get done.
